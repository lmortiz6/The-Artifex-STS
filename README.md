# The Artifex | Slay the Spire

This is a Slay the Spire mod based on the game Caves of Qud.

Place the jar in a mods folder in your Slay the Spire installation, and use ModtheSpire to play.
Requires BaseMod and STSLib.

## Description
This is a character mod based on the game Caves of Qud. Includes 75 cards and 25 relics.

You are a True Kin Artifex, a master of technological artifacts. Install cybernetic implants to permanently augment your abilities! Modify your cards with various effects! Utilize grenades, injectors, guns and ancient Eater tech! Will you run PunchKin? GunKin? ThrowKin?

### Cybernetics
Cybernetics are Power cards that can be permanently installed as Relics at Rest Sites. You are limited in how many you can install by your Cybernetic Credits. Perhaps if you were to find another credit wedge...

### Card Mods
Card mods grant additional effects to cards when played for the duration of combat. Make your attacks stronger, your skills more defensive, your Magnetized Chassis Plate more Springloaded.
